Privacy Policy

Welcome to Infinite Clipboard Manager. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Our extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Text you copy to your clipboard while using the Chrome browser.

No personal data, browsing history, or other sensitive information is collected or transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Data

The clipboard data is used solely to maintain a history of copied text for your convenience. This data is stored locally and is not shared or transmitted outside your device.

3. Data Storage

The clipboard history is stored locally in your browser's storage. The data is not synced to any cloud service or external server.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not sell, share, or provide your data to any third parties.

5. User Control

You can clear your clipboard history at any time by using the "Clear History" button in the extension. This will delete all stored clipboard data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this page periodically for any changes.
